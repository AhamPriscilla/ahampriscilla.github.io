I hope you liked this selection of my works.
I'm Aham Priscilla; an independent Digital illustrator & Designer currently based in Lagos.

My illustrations are inspired by witty expressions and the events everyday life  Being someone who learns and recalls information best through visual communication, each work I create reflects my visual understanding of these expressions in the most literal way possible.

While I specialize in digital paintings, illustrations, and design, you'll virtually always find me experimenting with new media. 
  I'm interested in working with individuals and businesses who want to use stunning  illustrations and designs to give their brand a fun and exciting look. Please don't hesitate to contact me if you'd like to say hi, or if you have any questions about my illustration services.
I'd love to hear from you.
